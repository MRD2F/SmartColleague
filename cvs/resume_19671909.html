Executive Profile
To obtain the job that continues to expand my administrative experience working with staff, students and parents to develop a shared vision and leading school community in achieving those goals. I believe that true leaders exhibit habits of mind and model the expectations they have of their employees. I believe two critical components of successful leadership are positive relationships and transparency.
