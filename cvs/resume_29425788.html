HEALTH COACH
Summary
Compassionate, results-oriented health psychologist with ten years of experience coaching individuals of various ethnic and socio-economic backgrounds and ranging in age from young adults to elders. Skilled in the use of the patient-centered, solution-focused principles of positive psychology, co-active coaching, the transtheoretical model of change, motivational interviewing, self-determination theory, and acceptance and commitment therapy to facilitate self-awareness and self-care. Dedicated coach with a demonstrated track record facilitating healthy lifestyle choices including smoking cessation, weight reduction, stress management, improved sleep, and enhanced daily functioning.
